Web Design Vs Web Development

Web Design and Web Development are two very important yet separate parts of the same problem. Let’s face it. There is a lot of technical-know how and expertise that goes into managing a modern day website. You have to worry about security issues, database management, inventory tracking, geo-location based responses and this doesn’t even begin to touch upon making the website look cool. While the opportunities for specialization are immense, developers generally divide themselves into two different groups. Those who are responsible for building the look and feel of the website, and the people who build all the internal technical components.

While front-end and back-end developers specialize in languages related to their field, they both rely upon the same back-bone of technologies. HTML, CSS and Javascript. According to the Github “Stats of the Octoverse 2018”, For the 8th year running, Javascript is the most popular programming language in the world by an enormous margin, underscoring its importance and versatility.
